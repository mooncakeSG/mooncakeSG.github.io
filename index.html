About Me

Hi, I'm Keawin Koesnel, a tech enthusiast and creative writer from South Africa. I have a strong passion for cybersecurity, law, and software development, and I'm currently expanding my knowledge in full-stack development.

I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Police Science at UNISA and have certifications in Cybersecurity. Alongside my studies, I'm part of the Kubernetes African Developer Training Program (KCNA) and the PLP Academy, where I'm honing my skills in Python, software development, SQL, and web technologies.

Beyond tech, I have a deep love for writing poetry and short stories, blending creativity with logic to build meaningful projects that empower communities.
